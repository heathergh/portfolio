About Heather Hamilton

Heather is a Web Developer and settler from the traditional territory of the Sḵwx̱wú7mesh (Squamish), Stó:lō and səlilwətaɬ (Tsleil-Waututh) and xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam) Nations (otherwise known as Vancouver).

She currently resides on Treaty 13 Lands, known as Toronto, where she has recently completed studies at Juno (formerly HackerYou) College of Technology for Front-End Web Development. She also holds a diploma in Full-Stack Web Development from Lighthouse Labs, which she received in 2017.

Heather has two years of professional experience, and has mentored at coding workshops organized by Canada Learning Code and Lighthouse Labs. She is currently seeking opportunities to build digital products using modern web technologies.

Prior to becoming a Web Developer, she completed her Bachelor of Arts in Geography at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver, Canada. After completing undergraduate studies, she worked in non-profits, law, and Fintech.
