About Heather Hamilton

Heather is a Front-End Developer and settler from the traditional territory of the Sḵwx̱wú7mesh (Squamish), Stó:lō and səlilwətaɬ (Tsleil-Waututh) and xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam) Nations (otherwise known as Vancouver)

She currently resides on Treaty 13 Lands known as Toronto, where she has recently completed studies at Juno College of Technology (formerly HackerYou) for Front-End Web Development.

Prior to becoming a Web Developer, she completed her Bachelor of Arts in Human Geography at Simon Fraser University, and worked in in non-profits, law and FinTech.

As a developer, she values creating beautiful, intuitive, and inclusive experiences for all users. She brings an analytical and creative approach to collaborative problem-solving as well as a love of learning and knowledge-sharing to her work as a developer.

She is currently seeking opportunities to build digital products using modern web technologies.
